Make Your Own Macro-Film

Below are different ways you can create your own Macro Video!

Abstract Video Art

Extreme Experimentation

Create abstract moving images by experimenting with dish liquid, milk, and paints!

  1. Step 1: Setup milk on a plate and phone with macro lens on a tripod.
  2. Step 2: Mix paints in small tubes/cups
  3. Step 3: Use a dropper to pick up the paints and dish liquid soap
  4. Step 4: Use the dropper with paint to compose your image on the milk
  5. Step 5: Use the dropper with Dish liquid to watch the magic happen!
  6. Last step: Don't forget to record!

Outdoor Schooting

Get close with nature, insects, things around you and see the beauty of the details we can't usually see with our eyes!

  1. Step 1: Setup Macro Lens on phone using the Lens Clip!
  2. Step 2: Explore the outdoors and look around for anything you wish to record!
  3. Step 3: Bring the Lens at a close distance to the object.
  4. Step 4: Ensure your camera is focused.
  5. Step 5: Capture the beauty of the outdoors!
